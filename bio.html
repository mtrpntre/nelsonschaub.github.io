Nelson Schaub was born in 1995, lives in la chaux de fonds and recently graduated from the WorkMaster program at HEAD. Their work focuses on mainstream cultural products, from their production to their consumption and their reappropriation. With these references, they explore diferent themes surrounding queerness, loneliness, creativity and escapism . Through performance, digital drawing, clothing design and music (under the pseudonym Être Peintre), they create dreamlike narrations where multiple personalities and characters meet eachother.
